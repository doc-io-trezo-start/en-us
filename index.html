What Is Trezór.io/Start?

Trezór.io/start is the official onboarding page for new Trezór users. It provides a step-by-step guide to set up your Trezór Model One or Trezór Model T. This includes installing the Trezór Suite, updating firmware, and backing up your recovery seed — the most critical part of your security setup. 🔑

Key Features You’ll Unlock

Once your device is set up, you’ll gain access to Trezór Suite, a powerful desktop app that offers:

  • Secure Portfolio Management: View your crypto balance, track transactions, and manage multiple wallets with ease.
  • Built-In Exchange: Buy, sell, and swap coins directly from the app without compromising security.
  • Passphrase Support: Add an extra layer of protection to your wallet with an optional passphrase feature.
  • Coin Control: Get advanced control over which UTXOs you send for enhanced privacy and fee management. 🧠
  • Tor Integration: For the privacy-focused, Trezór Suite supports Tor network routing for anonymous usage. 🌐

Advanced Tips for Power Users

If you’re ready to go beyond the basics, Trezór supports multi-sig wallets, integration with third-party apps like Electrum, and secure login to supported websites via FIDO2/U2F authentication. You can even run your own Bitcoin node and connect it to your Trezór Suite for maximum privacy. ⚙️
